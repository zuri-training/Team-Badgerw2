ABOUT US

Badgerw2 College is committed to preparing our students to meet the great challenges facing the state, nation and world. We apply our expertise as students, faculty, and staff to engage with communities across Ogun State.

Our mission is to produce excellent and outstanding students who can take up any challenges they encounter.

It is with pride that we declare that we've helped produce students who have participated in groundbreaking research, and community-engaged outreach that has not only helped the communities around us but the state of Ogun and in turn our country Nigeria.
